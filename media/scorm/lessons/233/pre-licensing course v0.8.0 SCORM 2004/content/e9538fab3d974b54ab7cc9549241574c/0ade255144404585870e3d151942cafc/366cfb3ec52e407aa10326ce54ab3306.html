The law requires that you turn your headlights on:

·30 minutes after sunset and leave them on until 30 minutes before sunrise. (Of course you'll want to be driving at the time; otherwise your battery will run out)
·Whenever your visibility is less than 1000 feet
·Whenever you are using your windshield wipers to clear rain, snow, sleet, etc

Even when headlights do not help you see in low light periods, they make it much easier for other drivers and pedestrians to see your vehicle

It is also illegal to drive with only your parking lights on
