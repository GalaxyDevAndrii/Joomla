• Using your senses and developing good perceptual skills
  • Making correct decisions and acting accordingly
  • Driving carefully to minimize errors
  • Making allowances for the lack of skills or bad attitudes of other drivers
  • Allowing for changing weather and road conditions or the actions of pedestrians and other drivers
  • Conceding the right of way when necessary to prevent a crash
  • Wearing a seat belt at all times, to keep the driver behind the wheel in order to control the vehicle
  • Maintaining a ‘space cushion’ to help recognize a crash-producing situation far enough in advance to prevent it
