• If you are being followed by a tailgater, you should change lanes or slow down to encourage them to pass
  • Don't take it personally
  • Stay calm and change lanes to let the tailgater pass or slow down to encourage them to pass
  • If the tailgater does not move along, find a well-lit public place or police station and pull off the road
  • Note: You are not as likely to be tailgated if you are driving in the right lane
