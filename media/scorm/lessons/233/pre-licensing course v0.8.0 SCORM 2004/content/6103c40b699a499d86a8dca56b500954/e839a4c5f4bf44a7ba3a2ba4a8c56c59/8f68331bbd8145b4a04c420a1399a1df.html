Typical Collision Factors

  • Speeding
  • Assuming they have the right-of-way
  • Improper turning
  • Disobeying stop signals and signs
  • Driving under the influence
  • Making unsafe lane changes
  • Driving on the wrong side of the road (unless you are in the UK)
  • Following too closely
  • Pedestrian violations
  • Unsafe starting and backing
  • Improper passing
  • Falling asleep behind the wheel
  • Equipment failure
  • Impeding traffic
