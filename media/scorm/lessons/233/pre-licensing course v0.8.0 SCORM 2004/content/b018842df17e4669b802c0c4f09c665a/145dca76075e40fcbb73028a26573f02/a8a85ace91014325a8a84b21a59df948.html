The purpose of the course is to help new drivers are ;

  • To help students to become safe and responsible drivers  
  • To reduce crashes, injuries, traffic violations and property damages
  • To develop a positive attitude toward defensive and safe driving
  • To learn how to fit safely into the transportation system with other highway users
  • To familiarize the students with the “rules-of-the-road”
  • To teach to the new drivers to show courtesy to other drivers when they are driving
