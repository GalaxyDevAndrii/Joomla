A permit holder may never drive:

  • Unless accompanied by a supervising driver at least age 21 who holds a valid drivers license for operating the vehicle you are driving
  • In a DMV road test area
  • On any street within a park in New York City 
  • Any Tunnel or Bridge under the jurisdiction of the Triborough Bridge and Tunnel Authority
  • On the Taconic State, Cross County, Saw Mill River, Hutchinson River parkways in Westchester County
